BEGINNER'S PROGRAM

Workouts for those who are just starting their fitness journey

BEGINNERS HOME ADVANCED

AB SERIES

QUICK CORE BURNER

This core burner is perfect for beginners, the exercises are not too difficult to perform so do take note of your form when doing these exercises. This workout is also very quick so there is no excuse not to do it!! It is 30 seconds of work time and 15 seconds of rest time. If you can, try not to take a longer rest to really feel the burn but if you need a longer rest the please do take it.

UPPER BODY SERIES

Burn Your Under Arm Fats

This workout is focused more on toning the arms with some easy exercises. When you are more comfortable with the exercises, you can try them out with dumbbells or shorten the rest time in between to make it harder for yourself. It is 30 seconds of work time and 15 seconds of rest time. Take note that you have to keep doing these exercises consistently to achieve results so don't give up so easily if you don't see results at the start.

LOWER BODY SERIES

Quick and Easy Butt and Leg Workout

This workout targets your glutes, quadriceps and inner thighs. The exercises are lower in intensity and focuses more on toning the targeted areas. As these are lower intensity exercises, try not to take too many breaks in between so that you feel the burn. Remember to do these exercises in a slow and controlled manner, don't rush through the reps. I hope this workout gives a nice burn for your lower body!!
